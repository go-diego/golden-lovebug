Poet. Writer. Educator.

Allyson Jeffredo is a poet, writer and educator originally from the Coachella Valley, but currently stationed in the Inland Empire. Her work can be found in numerous publications – both print and online. At the moment, she splits her time between teaching community college, community workshops and writing.
